Planner App

IndoJapan

IndoJapan is an educational platform with a specific focus on the Japanese language. The application boasts a unique advantage, namely a JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test) simulation designed to assist in preparing for employment opportunities in Japan. Additionally, the application offers various facilities and additional resources. It is planned to develop this application into a web-based platform to enhance accessibility for learning the Japanese language.

Wallpict

Wallpict is an Android application created using Android Studio, with Kotlin as its core programming language. This application provides a wide variety of wallpapers to enhance the appearance of your Android mobile device.
